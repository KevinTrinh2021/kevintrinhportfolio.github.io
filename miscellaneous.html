Miscellaneous page


In class, I worked on Model Of Creation Activity 4.2. On this project, I had about 2-3 days to make four models on Autodesk Inventor. The steps I did was I had to watch tutorial videos on the Schoology website, to learn on how to make these parts. From there, I learned how to start sketches, and make the parts by extruding additive or subtractive . Overall i enjoyed the project, and had no problems. At the end of this project, I learned the basics of Autodesk inventor and how to use it.
