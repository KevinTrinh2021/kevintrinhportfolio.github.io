Reverse Engineering

In This project, with help from an assigned team, my group and I reverse engineered an Orange bell. The Main purpose of this project was to learn more about your project that your reverse engineering and documenting/recording your data. Throughout this project, my team an I used Autodesk inventor to make our parts, used the dial caliper to record the dimensions and the Tripple beam balance tool. The whole project wasn't too complicated, all it needed was enough time to make these products through the computer CAD program. We first did the three main analysis for our orange bell. After that, we got all the needed dimensions to make the product on Inventor. Finally, after all parts have been made, we placed all our documentations in a presentation. From this project, I've learned why reverse engineering is important and how we can modify and change the parts to make it more advanced etc. Although the project took a lot of time, I did enjoy it and had no dislikes.
