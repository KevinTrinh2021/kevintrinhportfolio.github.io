Automata project

The Project Automata was a leap for us in our engineering and designing. On this project, I was assigned to a team of four people, where we had to design a specific cam with a specific diameter on auto desk inventor using about 2 weeks. When I was done with my cam model, I downloaded a box with a follower, allowing me to give a relationship to my cam and the box. When the relationship was added, I was able to rotate the cam at a certain degrees and measure the bottom and top of the follower to record on excel and make a histogram.Overall the project was pretty fun and I had no dislikes. From this project, I learned how to make/use an Automata, and increase my knowledge on creation and modeling.
