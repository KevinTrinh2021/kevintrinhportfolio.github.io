Architectural Styles

Welcome to my architectural styles page. This being one of the first projects to start off the CEA class year, each student drew a card displaying a type of architectural style. Drawing my card, I landed an international architectural style. The purpose of this project was to select a style type, research all about it, then compile a presentation over it and finally design and build our styles whether 2D or 3D. After researching about the time zone of my style as well as key features that make it stand out from other styles etc, Everyone one in my class had a couple minutes to display their presentations and record the data and info they found. Later on, our teacher assembled all our projects within the same room spaced out on multiple tables where all students had to assume which style was which. Overall, the project was very fun and simple. From this project, I learned many things from different architectural styles (even though we didn't cover every single style that exists) as well as getting exposed to what most of this class would feel like throughout the year.
