Design Elements & Principles

As of this assignment, everyone was to choose five objects around the school or classroom. When I chose my Five objects, I needed to do a visual analysis on them. During this project, I documented my visuals in my engineering notebook, and took pictures of them on my phone. and used a poster to hold all my printed out visual analysis sheets. The project was very simple and basic, first, I needed to choose five objects the I thought was appealing. Next, I documented each of the visual analysis of the parts in my notebook and pasted the pictures in a document and typed down there elements and principles. Finally, after printing out the documents, I glued the documents on a poster board. Overall the assignment wasn't too difficult. From this activity, I learned more about visuals on objects and the designs and principles that go with it. I had no dislikes on this assignment.
