Toy Train Project


The Purpose of this project was to create/design a toy train on a computerized
automated design software and a presentation over it to convince a group of toy
investors/manufacturers to use my toy train for their next toy train line. Through out
the whole project, I have used many constraints and criteria through this project. As
of the toy engine, tutorial videos were provided for us students, and the back car was
optional with no tutorials so you can design it the way you would like it to be. The
project wasn't to complex, through watching videos and tutorials with guidance from our
coach/Engineer teacher, we made our parts on Auto desk Inventor and assembled it all
together, made the proper dimension drawing sheets and exploded views and combined it
all together on a presentation slide show. Overall I actually thought this project was
very intresting and fun. From this lesson/project, my knowledge on the CAD program
has increased and no complaints.
